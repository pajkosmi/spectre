template<size_t Dim>
struct domain::Tags::GridToInertialInverseJacobian< Dim >

Computes the Grid to Inertial inverse Jacobian from CoordinatesVelocityAndJacobians. If the mesh is not moving, requesting this tag will throw an error because the Jacobian is just the identity.
