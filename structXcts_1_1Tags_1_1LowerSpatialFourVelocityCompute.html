Detailed Description

Computes \(u_i=W \gamma_{ij} v^j\), where \(W\) is the Lorentz factor, \(\gamma_{ij}\) is the spatial metric, and \(v^j\) is the spatial velocity.

This compute item is intended for observations in a pure XCTS solve where the hydro quantities are retrieved directly from the background solution/data.
