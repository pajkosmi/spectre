Detailed Description

Data determined by PrimitiveRecoverySchemes at a single grid point.

rho_h_w_squared is \(\rho h W^2\) where \(\rho\) is the rest mass density, \(h\) is the specific enthalpy, and \(W\) is the Lorentz factor.
