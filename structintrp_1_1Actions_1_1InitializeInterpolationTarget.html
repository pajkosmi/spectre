template<typename Metavariables, typename InterpolationTargetTag>

Initializes an InterpolationTarget.

Uses: nothing

    • Tags::Variables<typename InterpolationTargetTag::vars_to_interpolate_to_target>
  • Removes: nothing
  • Modifies: nothing
