 avg_distorted_normal_dot_unit_coord_vector is the average of distorted_normal_dot_unit_coord_vector over the excision boundary. Here distorted_normal_dot_unit_coord_vector is Eq. 93 of [79]. distorted_normal_dot_unit_coord_vector is \(\hat{n}_i x^i/r\) where \(\nat{n}_i\) is the distorted-frame unit normal to the excision boundary (pointing INTO the hole, i.e. out of the domain), and \(x^i/r\) is the distorted-frame (or equivalently the grid frame because it is invariant between these two frames because of the required limiting behavior of the map we choose) Euclidean normal vector from the center of the excision-boundary Strahlkorper to each point on the excision-boundary Strahlkorper.
