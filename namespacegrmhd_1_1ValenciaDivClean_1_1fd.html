Reconstructs the PrimTagsForReconstruction and if compute_conservatives is true computes the Lorentz factor, upper spatial velocity, specific internal energy, specific enthalpy, and the conserved variables.

Reconstructs the PrimTagsForReconstruction (usually \(\rho, p, Wv^i, B^i\), and \(\Phi\)), and if compute_conservatives is true computes the Lorentz factor, upper spatial velocity, specific internal energy, specific enthalpy, and the conserved variables.

All results are written into vars_on_lower_face and vars_on_upper_face.

The reason the PrimTagsForReconstruction can be specified separately is because some variables might need separate reconstruction methods from others, e.g. to guarantee the reconstructed solution is positive.
