     256             :  * This class implements background data for the XCTS equations describing two
     257             :  * objects in a quasi-equilibrium orbit, i.e. with \f$\bar{u}=0\f$ and

     274             :  * window functions. The window functions

     276             :  * at the position of the other is strongly damped, and they also avoid
     277             :  * logarithmic scaling of the solution at large distances where we would

     289             :  * gravitational influence of the other body into account. Therefore, the matter
